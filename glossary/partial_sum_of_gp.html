If a geometric progression has first term a and common ratio r, then the sum of the first n terms of the progression is $ \mathrm{s}_{\mathrm{n}} = \frac{\mathrm{a} ( 1 - \mathrm{r}^{\mathrm{n}} ) } { 1 - \mathrm{r}} . $

The 13rd uniform polyhedron is the Truncated octahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 2 4|3. Its dual is the tetrakishexahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (6,6,4); its symmetry group is the octahedral group S4.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 24

Number of edges: 36

Number of faces: 14 (=8 hexagons and 6 quadrilaterals).

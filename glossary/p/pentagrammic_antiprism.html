The 4th uniform polyhedron is the Pentagrammic antiprism; it has Wythoff symbol |2 2 5/2. Its dual is the pentagrammic deltohedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (3,3,3,5/2); its symmetry group is the dihedral group D5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 2

Number of vertices: 10

Number of edges: 20

Number of faces: 12 (=10 triangles and 25/2).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

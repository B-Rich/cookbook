A type of matrix defined by a column vector c and a row vector r. If c has elements ci, i = 1...m, andrhaselementsri,  i=1...n, then the Toeplitz matrix defined by them, T, is an n × m matrix with elements
$$t_{i,j} = \left \{ \begin{array}{cc} c_{i-j+1}, & i-j \geq 0 \\ r_{j-i+1}, & j-i > 0 . \end{array} \right .$$
eg. if $ c= \left ( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{array} \right ) $ and $ r= \left ( \begin{array}{cccc} 6 & 7 & 8 & 9 \end{array} \right ) , $ then $ T = \left ( \begin{array}{ccccc} 1 & 6 & 7 & 8 & 9 \\ 2 & 1 & 6 & 7 & 8 \\ 3 & 2 & 1 & 6 & 7 \\ 4 & 3 & 2 & 1 & 6 \\ 5 & 4 & 3 & 2 & 1 \end{array} \right ). $

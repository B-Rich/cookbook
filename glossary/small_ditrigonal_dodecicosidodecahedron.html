The 48th uniform polyhedron is the Small ditrigonal dodecicosidodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 5/3 3|5. Its dual is the small ditrigonal dodecacronic hexecontahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (10,5/3,10,3); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -16

Density: 4

Number of vertices: 60

Number of edges: 120

Number of faces: 44 (=1210and 20 triangles and 125/3).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

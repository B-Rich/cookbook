1/4 turn clockwise is the same as 3/4 turn anticlockwise;

1/2 turn clockwise is the same as 1/2 turn anticlockwise.

In general, a fraction f of a turn clockwise is the same as a fraction (1-f) of a turn anticlockwise.

In degrees, this is expressed by subtracting the size of the turn from 360 ∘ . For instance, 180 ∘  clockwise = 180 ∘  anticlockwise, 181 ∘  clockwise = 179 ∘  anticlockwise, etc.

An imperial measure of mass that is sometimes used today.

16 ounces make a pound, another imperial unit used for measuring mass. This would be written: 16 oz = 1lb.

Your parents may have gone shopping for four ounces of sweets. If they did they would have asked the shopkeeper for a quarter, meaning a quarter of a pound, of wine gums or toffees...

An ounce is equal to about 28.34952g.

We can find the area under a function f(x) between endpoints x=a and x=b in the following way:

Divide the interval [a, b] into n parts by points x0, x1,  ... xn, withx0 = aandxn = b. The subintervals have lengths x1 − x0...xn − xn − 1. Pick a point tiineachinterval[xi, xi + 1].

Then a Riemann Sum is
R = ∑i = 0n − 1(xi + 1 − xi)f(ti)
Then we can define ∫abf(x)dx as the limit of R as the size of the largest subinterval goes to zero.

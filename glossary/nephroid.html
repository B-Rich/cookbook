An epicycloid with two cusps. It is formed as the locus of a point on a circle of radius a rolling round outside a circle of radius 2a.

It has parametric equations x=a(3.cos(t)-3.cos(3t)), y=a(3.sin(t)-sin(3t)).

It is the shape formed when light is reflected from the inside of a semicircle; sometimes you can see this on the surface of a cup of liquid.

It is the envelope of circles with their centres on a given circle, touching a given diameter of that circle.

The evolute of a nephroid is a nephroid.

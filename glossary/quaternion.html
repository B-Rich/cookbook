An ordered set of four numbers. Sometimes a quaternion is written in the form x0 + ix1 + jx2 + kx3;  sometimes it is considered to be a scalar and a three-component vector.

Quaternions written as above can be added and multiplied, using the laws:


$$\begin{aligned} i^{2} & = j^{2} = & k^{2} = -1 \\ ij & = k = & -ji \\ jk & = i = & -kj \\ ki & = j = & -ik.\end{aligned}$$

The set of quaternions is a 4-dimensional vector space over R. A basis of this space is 1, i, j, k.

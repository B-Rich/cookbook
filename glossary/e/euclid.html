Euclid:

A Greek mathematician(born c. 300BC) famous for his work on Geometry, especially his book called The Elements. He devised a geometry dealing with points, lines and planes based on some specific assumptions, that:

  • a line can go through any two points

  • a line can be extended indefinitely in both directions

  • all right angles are equal

  • one line only can be drawn parallel to a given line through a point not on the line.

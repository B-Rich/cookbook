A set of elements which are part of a larger set. A subset can be the whole or part of the larger set, or the empty set.

The set of pupils who play the recorder includes: Anna, Claire, David, Diane, Emma, John, Kate, Kip, Liam, Sally, Tom,

one subset is; David, John, Kip, Liam,Tom,

another is: David, Diane,

another is: - the empty set.

The 51st uniform polyhedron is the Snub icosidodecadodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol |5/3 3 5. Its dual is the medial hexagonal hexecontahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (3,5/3,3,3,3,5); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -16

Density: 4

Number of vertices: 60

Number of edges: 180

Number of faces: 104 (=12 pentagons and 80 triangles and 125/3).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

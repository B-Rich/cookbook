We multiply a vector or matrix by a scalar by multiplying each element of the vector or matrix by that scalar. Eg:
$$5 \left ( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 3 \\ 5 \end{array} \right ) = \left ( \begin{array}{c} 5 \times 1 \\ 5 \times 3 \\ 5 \times 5 \end{array} \right ) = \left ( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ 15 \\ 25 \end{array} \right )$$

$$2 \left ( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 3 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right ) = \left ( \begin{array}{cc} 2 \times 4 & 2 \times 3 \\ 2 \times 1 & 2 \times 2 \end{array} \right ) = \left ( \begin{array}{cc} 8 & 6 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right )$$

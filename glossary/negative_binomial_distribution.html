A discrete distribution defined by


P(X = r) = r − 1Cn − 1qr − npn, 
r=n, n+1, ..., p in (0,1), q=1-p.

It has mean n/p and variance nq/p2.

It describes the number of trials needed to obtain n successes in a binomial process. The special case n=1 is the geometric distribution.

It has PGF [pt/(1 − qt)]n.

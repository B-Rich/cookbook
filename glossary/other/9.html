Nine is the third square number, and the sum of two cubes: 1+8. It is the first odd number that is not prime, apart from 1.

8, 9 is the only pair of prime powers which differ by 1.

It is the only square number to be the sum of two consecutive cubes: 13 + 23 = 9.

It is the fourth Lucky number, and the first Lucky number after 1 which is a square.

It is the second Kaprekar number (after 1).

It is the fourth subfactorial number.

ϕ(9) = 6,  d(9) = 3,  σ(9) = 13.

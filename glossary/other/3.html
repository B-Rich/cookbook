Three is equal to two plus one; it is the second prime number and the second triangular number. It is a Fermat prime, as it is equal to 220 + 1. 3 is number of dimensions of space in the everyday world. A number is divisible by three if (and only if) the sum of its digits is divisible by 3. It is the second Lucky number. 3 is the first Mersenne prime: 3 = 22 − 1. ϕ(3) = 2,  d(3) = 2,  σ(3) = 4.

The 54th uniform polyhedron is the Small icosihemidodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 3/2 3|5. Its dual is the small icosihemidodecacron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (10,3/2,10,3); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -4

It is one-sided; it has

Number of vertices: 30

Number of edges: 60

Number of faces: 26 (=610and 10 triangles and 103/2).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

For data consisting of n-tuples of values (x1...xn),  we can compute the covariance Cij of any pair of variables xi, xj. The matrix $ C = \left( \begin{array}{ccc} C_{11} & \ldots & C_{1n} \\ \vdots & & \vdots \\ C_{n1} & \ldots & C_{nn} \end{array} \right) $ whose entries are these covariances, is called the covariance matrix for the data.

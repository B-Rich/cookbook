Abbreviation: UFD. An integral domain R in which every a ≠ 0 has a factorisation a = up1…pk , unique up to units u and permutation of primes p1, …, pk (note that in a UFD, the notions of primeness and irreducibility are identical).

Every principal ideal domain is a UFD, but not vice versa, e.g. Z[X] is a UFD but not a PID, as ⟨2, X⟩ is not a principal ideal.

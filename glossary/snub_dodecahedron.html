The 34th uniform polyhedron is the Snub dodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol | 2 3 5. Its dual is the pentagonal hexecontahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (3,3,3,3,5); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

2

Density:

1

Number of vertices:

60

Number of edges:

150

Number of faces:

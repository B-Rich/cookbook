A number whose digits are all units, i.e. 11, 111, etc.

The notation Rn is used for the repunit with n digits.

No repunit can be a square, but it is not known if one can be a cube. Very few of them are prime (R2, R23,  R19andR317 are the only known prime repunits), but it is not known whether there are infinitely many prime repunits.

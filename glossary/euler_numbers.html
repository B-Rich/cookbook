Numbers occurring in the power series expansions of sech(x) and sec(x):
$$\mathrm{sech(x)} = 1 - \frac{\mathrm{x}}{2} + \frac{\mathrm{E}_1 \mathrm{x}^2} {2!} - \frac{\mathrm{E}_2 \mathrm{x}^4} {4!} + \frac{\mathrm{E}_3 \mathrm{x}^6} {6!} - ...$$

$$\mathrm{sec(x)} = \frac{\mathrm{E}_1 \mathrm{x}^2}{2!} + \frac{\mathrm{E}_2 \mathrm{x}^4}{4!} + \frac{\mathrm{E}_3 \mathrm{x}^6}{6!} + ...$$
Ei are the Euler numbers.

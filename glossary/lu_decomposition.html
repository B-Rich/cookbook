Any square matrix A can be written as the product of a lower triangular matrix L and an upper triangular matrix U. This is called an LU decomposition or LU factorisation. Eg:
$$\left ( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 3 \\ -10 & -18 \end{array} \right ) = \left ( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ -5 & 1 \end{array} \right ) \left ( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 3 \\ 0 & -3 \end{array} \right )$$

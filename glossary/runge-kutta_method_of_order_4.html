A method for the numerical solution of first order differential equations: given equation $ \frac{dy}{dx} = f(x,y) $ and a starting point x0, y0, values of yi + 1 for xi = xi + h are given approximately by yi + 1 = yi + (k1 + 2k2 + 2k3 + k4),  where


$$\begin{aligned} k_{1}&=&hf(x_{i}, y_{i})\\, k_{2}&=&hf(x_{i}+h/2, y_{i}+k_{1}/2),\\ k_{3}&=&hf(x_{i}+h/2, y_{i}+k_{2}/2),\\ k_{4}&=&hf(x_{i}+h, y_{i}+k_{3}). \end{aligned}$$

A set F with two commutative and associative operations, + and  × ,  such that:

1)if a and b are in F then a+b and a × b are in F;

2)+ has an identity, 0, and  ×  has an identity, 1, and these are not the same;

3)every element has an inverse under +, and every element but 0 has an inverse under  × ; 

4) ×  is distributive over +.

Q,R,C are all fields with the operations of addition and multiplication.

The definition can also be written: F is a commutative group under addition; F/0 is a commutative group under multiplication; multiplication is distributive over addition.

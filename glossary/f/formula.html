A shorthand way of writing down a rule, an equation showing the connection between related quantities.

eg The area of a rectangle (A) with side lengths L and B, is given by the formula: A = L ⋅ B.

The perimeter of a square (P) with side length a, is given by: P = 4a

The volume of a cuboid (V) with side lengths l, b and h is given by: V = l ⋅ b ⋅ h.

The plural of formula is formulae.

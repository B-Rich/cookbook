If q is any positive real number, the Fejer kernel is
$$\mathrm{F}_{\mathrm{q}} ( \theta ) = \left \{ \begin{array}{cc} \mathrm{q} & \theta = 0, \pm 2 \pi , \pm 4 \pi, ... \\ \frac{1}{\mathrm{q}} \left( \frac{\mathrm{sin(q} \theta / 2)} {\mathrm{sin} ( \theta / 2 ) } \right )^2 & \mathrm{otherwise} \end{array} \right .$$

This is used in the analysis of series and spectra.

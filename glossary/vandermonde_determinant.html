For a given set of numbers x1...xn,  the Vandermonde determinant is:
$$\left | \begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 1 & ... & 1 \\ x_1 & x_2 & ... & x_n \\ x_1^2 & x_2^2 & ... & x_n^2 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ x_1^{n-1} & x_2 ^{n-1} & ... & x_n^{n-1} \end{array} \right | .$$
It is equal to the product of all differences between pairs of the numbers: ∏1 ≤ i < j ≤ n(xj − xi).

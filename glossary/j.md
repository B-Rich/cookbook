$\vec{j}$ is conventionally used to denote the unit vector in the $y$
direction: $ \vec{j} = \left ( 
  \begin{array}{c} 
    0 \\
    1 
  \end{array} 
  \right ) $ in two dimensions or $ \vec{j} = \left ( 
  \begin{array}{c} 
    0 \\ 
    1 \\
    0 
  \end{array} 
  \right ) $ in three dimensions.

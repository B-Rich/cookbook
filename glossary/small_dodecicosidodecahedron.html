The 38th uniform polyhedron is the Small dodecicosidodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 3/2 5|5. Its dual is the small dodecacronic hexecontahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (10,3/2,10,5); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -16

Density: 2

Number of vertices: 60

Number of edges: 120

Number of faces: 44 (=1210and 12 pentagons and 203/2).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

The 29th uniform polyhedron is the Icosidodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 2|3 5. Its dual is the rhombic triacontahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (3,5,3,5); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 30

Number of edges: 60

Number of faces: 32 (=12 pentagons and 20 triangles).

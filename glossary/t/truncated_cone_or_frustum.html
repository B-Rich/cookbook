What is left when a cone or pyramid has its top sliced off.

Often the slice has been made parallel to the base of the cone or pyramid.

Many household items are frusta of particular solids.

Yoghurt pots are frusta of cones.

Some sweet containers are frusta of pyramids.

The spelling frustum is more usually considered to be correct today, although frustrum is still widely used, particularly when making the plural - frustra.

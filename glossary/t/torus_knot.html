A knot produced by winding a curve around a torus.

If we consider a torus to be formed as the product of two circles A and B (we form the torus by rotating B around the circumference of A) then a torus knot is produced by winding a curve p times around A and q times around B. Such a knot is denoted Tp, q. The trefoil is a specific case of this: T2, 3.

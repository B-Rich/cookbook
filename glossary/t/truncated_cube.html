The 14th uniform polyhedron is the Truncated cube; it has Wythoff symbol 2 3|4. Its dual is the triakisoctahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (8,8,3); its symmetry group is the octahedral group S4.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 24

Number of edges: 36

Number of faces: 14 (=6 octagons and 8 triangles).

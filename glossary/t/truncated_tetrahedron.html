The 7th uniform polyhedron is the Truncated tetrahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 2 3|3. Its dual is the triakis tetrahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (6,6,3); its symmetry group is the tetrahedral group A4.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 12

Number of edges: 18

Number of faces: 8 (=4 hexagons and 4 triangles).

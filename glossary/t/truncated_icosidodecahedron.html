The 33rd uniform polyhedron is the Truncated icosidodechedon; it has Wythoff symbol 2 3 5|. Its dual is the disdyakistriacontahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (4,6,10); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 120

Number of edges: 180

Number of faces: 62 (=1210and 20 hexagons and 30 quadrilaterals).

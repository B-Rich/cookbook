A method for finding the sum of certain series.

If we have a series an,  and we can find a function f(n) such that an = f(n + 1) − f(n) for every n, then
∑i = 0nai = ∑i = 0nf(i+1)-f(i)

 = f(n+1)-f(0)
(because all the other terms cancel).

But finding such a function may be difficult, and is not always even possible.

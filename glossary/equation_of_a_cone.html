A (right circular) cone whose base is a circle of radius r centred at the origin, lying in the x, y plane, and whose axis is in the z-direction with length h, can be described by two formulas. The circular base has z = 0, x2 + y2 < r2. The curved surface has x2 + y2 = (r − (rz/h))2,  for z in the range [0, h].

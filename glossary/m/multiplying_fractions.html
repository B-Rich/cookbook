To multiply two fractions, we write them side by side; to get the top number of the answer we multiply together the two top numbers, and to get the bottom number of the answer we multiply together the two bottom numbers.

For example, $ \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{5} . $

Multiplying the two top numbers gives 8. Multiplying the two bottom numbers gives 15. So the answer is $ \frac{8}{15} $

i.e. $ \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{5} = \frac{2 \times 4}{3 \times 5} = \frac{8}{15} . $

The vector with x and y components $ \frac {\partial \mathrm{f}}{\partial \mathrm{x}} $ and $ \frac {\partial \mathrm{f}}{\partial \mathrm{y}} . $

This is often written grad(f) or ∇f.

The rate of change of f at (x,y) as we move in direction (u,v) is given by ∇f(x,y) ⋅ (u,v).

∇f is normal to surfaces on which f is constant.

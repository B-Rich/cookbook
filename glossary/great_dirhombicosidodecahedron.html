The 80th uniform polyhedron is the Great dirhombicosidodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol |3/2 5/3 3 5/2. Its dual is the great dirhombicosidodecacron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (4,5/3,4,3,4,5/2,4,3/2); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -56

It is two-sided; it has

Number of vertices: 60

Number of edges: 240

Number of faces: 124 (=60 quadrilaterals and 20 triangles and 125/2and 125/3and 203/2).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

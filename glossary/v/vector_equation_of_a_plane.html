A plane containing the point ǎ and having direction vectors b and č has equation x = a + tb + uc, where t and u may be any real numbers.

Note that b and č must not be parallel to one another.

Alternatively: given a point A with position vector ǎ and a normal vector ň, the plane perpendicular to ň through ǎ has vector equation (r − a).n = 0.

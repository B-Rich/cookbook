A special kind of Taylor series, of the form:
f(x) = f(0) + f′(0)x + f′′(0)x2/2 + f′′′(0)x3/3! + ... + f(n − 1)(0)xn − 1/(n − 1)! + Rn.
(This is a Taylor series with a = 0.) Maclaurin’s theorem states the conditions under which such a series exists for a function f. Rn is a remainder term, and can be written in the same forms as that of a Taylor series.

The 31st uniform polyhedron is the Truncated dodecahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 2 3|5. Its dual is the triakisicosahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (10,10,3); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 60

Number of edges: 90

Number of faces: 32 (12 decagons and 20 triangles).

The 15th uniform polyhedron is the Rhombicuboctahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 3 4|2. Its dual is the deltoidal icositetrahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (4,3,4,4); its symmetry group is the octahedral group S4.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 24

Number of edges: 48

Number of faces: 26 (=18 quadrilaterals and 8 triangles).

The fourth counting number; it is the second square number, as it is equal to 2 × 2 or 22 (interestingly, it is also equal to 2+2).

It is also the first tetrahedral number, and the first composite number.

4 is the smallest number which is the square of a prime number.

It is the number of faces or vertices of a tetrahedron, the simplest polyhedron.

It is the only composite number n which does not divide (n-1)!

ϕ(4) = 2,  d(4) = 3,  σ(4) = 7.

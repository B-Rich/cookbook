28 is a happy number because:

28 → 22 + 82 = 4 + 64 = 68

68 → 62 + 82 = 36 + 64 = 100

100 → 12 + 02 + 02 = 1

Twenty eight can be reduced to 1 by the process of repeatedly squaring and adding the digits. Thirty two is also a happy number:

32 → 32 + 22 = 9 + 4 = 13

13 → 12 + 32 = 1 + 9 = 10

10 → 12 + 02 = 1 + 0 = 1

But 24 is a sad, unhappy number. Can you see why?

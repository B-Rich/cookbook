Let K be a number field and n = [K : Q] = r1 + 2r2 where r1 is the number of real embeddings $ K \hookrightarrow \mathbb{C} $ , and r2 the number of pairs of complex embeddings $ K \hookrightarrow \mathbb{C} $ . Then
OK *  ≅ μ(K) × Zr1 + r2 − 1, 
where μ(K) is the set of roots of unity in K .

Put another way, this means that there are r = r1 + r2 − 1 “fundamental units” ɛ1, …, ɛr such that any unit u ∈ OK *  can be written uniquely as
u = ζɛ1n1…ɛrnr
for some ζ ∈ μ(K) .

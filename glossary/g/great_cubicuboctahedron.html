The 19th uniform polyhedron is the Great cubicuboctahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 3 4|4/3. Its dual is the great hexacronic icositetrahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (8/3,3,8/3,4); its symmetry group is the octahedral group S4.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -4

Density: 4

Number of vertices: 24

Number of edges: 48

Number of faces: 20 (=6 quadrilaterals and 8 triangles and 68/3).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

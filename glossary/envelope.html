A curve or a surface which touches every member of a family of lines, curves, planes or surfaces.

A family of curves in two dimensions f(x,y,t)=0 (where t is a parameter) has an envelope whose equation can be found by solving simultaneously:


$$\begin{aligned} f(x,y,p)&=&0 \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial p } f(x,y,p) &=& 0\end{aligned}$$

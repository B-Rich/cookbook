If F(x) is a vector function, and F = (Fx, Fy, Fz),  then curl F, written ∇ ∧ F,  is given by:
$$\left( \begin{array}{c} \frac{\partial \mathrm{F}_{\mathrm{z}}}{\partial \mathrm{y}} - \frac{\partial \mathrm{F}_{\mathrm{y}}}{\partial \mathrm{z}} \\ \frac{\partial \mathrm{F}_{\mathrm{x}}}{\partial \mathrm{z}} - \frac{\partial \mathrm{F}_{\mathrm{z}}}{\partial \mathrm{x}} \\ \frac{\partial \mathrm{f}_{\mathrm{y}}}{\partial \mathrm{x}} - \frac{\partial \mathrm{F}_{\mathrm{x}}}{\partial \mathrm{y}} \end{array} \right ).$$

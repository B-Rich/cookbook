Cauchy’s theorem for triangles: let D be a domain and f : D → C be holomorphic. Then if T is a triangle lying entirely in D ,
∫∂Tf(z)dz = 0, 
where ∂T is the boundary of T . Compare Morera’s theorem.

Cauchy’s theorem for a star domain: let D be a star domain and f : D → C be holomorphic. Then
∫φf(z)dz = 0
for all closed paths φ in D .

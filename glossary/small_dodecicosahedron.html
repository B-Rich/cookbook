The 55th uniform polyhedron is the Small dodecicosahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 3/2 3 5|. Its dual is the small dodecicosacron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (10,6,10/9,6/5); its symmetry group is the icosahedral group A5.

It has:

Euler characteristic: -28

It is one-sided; it has

Number of vertices: 60

Number of edges: 120

Number of faces: 32 (=610and 10 hexagons and 106/5and 610/9).

Note: a polygon a/b means a shape made by taking a points and joining every bth of them; for instance 5/2 means a five-pointed star made by taking five points and joining every second point.

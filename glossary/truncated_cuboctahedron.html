The 16th uniform polyhedron is the Truncated cuboctahedron; it has Wythoff symbol 2 3 4|. Its dual is the disdyakisdodecahedron. The pattern of polygons round each vertex is (4,6,8); its symmetry group is the octahedral group S4.

It has:

Euler characteristic: 2

Density: 1

Number of vertices: 48

Number of edges: 72

Number of faces: 26 (=6 octagons and 8 hexagons and 12 quadrilaterals).
